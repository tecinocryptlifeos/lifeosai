Contact

Contact the LifeOS AI project.

Use the appropriate channel for product support, business communication, privacy requests or responsible issue reporting.

Product support

When reporting a problem, include the page you were using, the action you attempted, the exact message shown and the device or browser involved. Do not send passwords, secret API keys, payment credentials or unnecessary personal records.

Business and community enquiries

LifeOS AI welcomes serious enquiries concerning responsible partnerships, public-interest education, community decision support and the ethical use of synthetic intelligence. Any proposed collaboration should state its objective, intended audience and expected public value.

Privacy and data requests

Privacy, access or deletion requests should clearly identify the relevant account or session information without including sensitive credentials. The project may request reasonable verification before acting on a data request in order to protect users from unauthorised disclosure.
