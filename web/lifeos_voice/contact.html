Contact and Support

Contact the LifeOS AI support team.

Use the official email channel for product support, business communication, privacy requests or responsible issue reporting. Review the frequently asked questions before sending a support request.

Product support

When reporting a problem, include the page you were using, the action you attempted, the exact message shown and the device or browser involved. Do not send passwords, secret API keys, payment credentials or unnecessary personal records.

Business and community enquiries

LifeOS AI welcomes serious enquiries concerning responsible partnerships, public-interest education, community decision support and the ethical use of synthetic intelligence. Any proposed collaboration should state its objective, intended audience and expected public value.

Privacy and data requests

Privacy, access or deletion requests should clearly identify the relevant account or session information without including sensitive credentials. The project may request reasonable verification before acting on a data request in order to protect users from unauthorised disclosure.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a technical problem?

Send the page address, the action you attempted, the exact message shown, and the device and browser you used. Do not include passwords, secret keys, payment credentials or unrelated private records.

Where should I send a support request?

Email lifeos.crwnmdtc@gmail.com with a clear subject line and only the information needed to investigate the request.

Can LifeOS AI guarantee the result of a decision?

No. LifeOS AI provides decision support and structured analysis. Important facts and high-stakes conclusions must be independently verified.

Can I request a partnership or community project?

Yes. Explain the objective, intended audience, expected public value, proposed responsibilities and relevant timeframe in the email.

How do I make a privacy or data request?

State the nature of the request and the relevant account or session information without sending passwords or authentication codes. Reasonable verification may be required.

LifeOS Synthetic Intelligence supports clearer human decisions; it does not replace qualified professional judgement.
