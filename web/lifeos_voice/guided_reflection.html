LifeOS process · Stage three

Continue Guided Reflection

Use disciplined follow-up questions to test assumptions, evidence, emotion and neglected perspectives.

Challenge the first answer

Test whether the stated reason is complete and supported.

  • What am I assuming?
  • What contradicts me?
  • What am I avoiding?

Compare perspectives

Consider the issue from the position of affected people, a neutral observer and your future self.

  • Affected person
  • Neutral observer
  • Future self

Interpret emotion responsibly

Treat emotion as information rather than automatic instruction.

  • Fear
  • Anger
  • Excitement

Know when analysis is sufficient

End reflection when material facts, risks and duties are sufficiently understood.

  • Decision threshold
  • Remaining uncertainty
  • Review point
