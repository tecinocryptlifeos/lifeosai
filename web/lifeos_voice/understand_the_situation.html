LifeOS process · Stage one

Understand the Real Situation

Clarify facts, pressure, affected people, assumptions and uncertainty before choosing a direction.

Define the decision

State the real choice in one clear sentence and separate it from surrounding problems.

  • Decision owner
  • Real alternatives
  • Decision deadline

Recognise who is affected

Identify the people, relationships and communities that may carry the effect of the decision.

  • Direct effect
  • Indirect effect
  • Long-term obligation

Separate facts from assumptions

Distinguish verified information from expectation, fear or preference.

  • Known facts
  • Critical unknowns
  • Testable assumptions

Test urgency

Determine whether immediate action is genuinely required or emotionally imposed.

  • Real deadline
  • Cost of delay
  • Cost of haste
