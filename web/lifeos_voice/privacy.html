Privacy Policy

Privacy information for LifeOS AI users.

This policy explains the categories of information that may be processed when you use the public website, Decision Chat or voice features.

Information you provide

LifeOS AI may process text entered into Decision Chat, voice input converted or transmitted for conversation processing, follow-up questions and technical support information. Do not submit passwords, authentication codes, payment credentials, government identification records or confidential information you are not authorised to share.

Technical information

The hosting service and connected providers may process standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, request time, device characteristics, error information and security logs. This information can be necessary to deliver the service, prevent abuse and diagnose failures.

AI and voice providers

Decision and voice requests may be transmitted to third-party artificial-intelligence or speech-service providers configured by LifeOS AI. Those providers process the supplied information to generate a response. Users should review this policy before using sensitive scenarios and should avoid submitting information that is unnecessary for the decision.

Local conversation information

Some conversation history and settings may be stored in the user’s browser through local storage. Clearing browser data, using the available clear controls or changing devices may remove or separate that local information. Local browser storage is not the same as a permanent cross-device account.

Cookies, analytics and advertising

The public content area may later use cookies, analytics or Google advertising services. Where required, users will be given relevant notice or consent controls. Google and other advertising vendors may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure advertisements according to their own policies and applicable law. Advertising will not be intentionally placed inside private decision or voice conversation screens.

Retention and security

Information is retained only for legitimate operational, security, legal or service purposes according to the systems in use. No internet service can promise absolute security. LifeOS AI uses reasonable controls and limits access, but users must also protect their devices and avoid submitting unnecessary sensitive information.

Your choices

Users may stop using the service, clear available browser history controls and request information about applicable access or deletion procedures through the official contact route. Legal rights differ by location and may require identity verification.

Children

LifeOS AI is not designed to collect personal information from children without appropriate parental or guardian involvement. Guardians should supervise use and should not allow a child to submit sensitive personal information.

Policy changes

This policy may be updated when the service, providers, law or data practices change. The current version published on this page governs the public website. Last updated: 30 June 2026.
